About Me

I’m Jonathan, founder and CEO of EFL AG-DATA, a market-data and analytics company focused on the feed and livestock industries across China and Southeast Asia. With over 16 years of industry and research experience, I build data systems, insights, and digital tools that help feed-additive companies, integrators, and agribusinesses make faster, more informed decisions.

I’m passionate about transforming agriculture through reliable data, practical technology, and solutions that actually work for people on the ground. My work combines a deep understanding of livestock markets with a hands-on approach to building platforms and partnerships. I enjoy connecting with people across the industry to share insights, explore ideas, and create impactful solutions together.
